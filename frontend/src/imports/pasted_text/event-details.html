Regional Sustainable Development Summit 2024: Innovation in Mountainous Regions

Sustainability International Relations

info Event Overview

The 2024 Summit brings together global thought leaders, regional policy makers, and innovative researchers to discuss the unique challenges and opportunities for sustainable growth in Central Asia's mountainous territories.

Focusing on three core pillars—Economic Resilience, Environmental Preservation, and Social Equity—this three-day event will feature collaborative workshops, keynote addresses from international experts, and high-level panel discussions designed to catalyze actionable change.
